World Total Electricity Consumption until now

It visualizes the total electricity consumption around the world. In this visualization, there is color hue used for the color choice. When the country consumes less electricity, then the color will be in light green. Likewise, the higher the electricity consumption, the darker the color it has such as from yellow to dark red. Data Transformation is applied to calculate the electricity consumption. The electricity consumption is normalized by the overall population. The range is [0, 2, 5, 11]. Blue area means the place is in ocean and there is graticule showing in the map. Moreover, the color in grey means that there are no related data from the dataset from the greyscale country. In this idiom, there is high electricity consumption in United States, Canada and Australia. Let's explore why these country have higher electricity consumption than other countries.


Renewable Energy Production around the world

This map idiom visualises the renewable energy production around the world. Renewable Energy included solar electricity, hydro electricity, wind electricity and other renewable electricity. In this visualisation, countries like Canada, Norway and Sweden is the world leaders that generates the highest renewable energy. Likewise, most countries in Africa does not much rely on the renewable energy. This is because the highly cost of implementing renewable energy technologies in their countries.


Global Electricity Consumption per Country

This scatterplot visualise the relationship between total electricity consumption and GDP per capita. The higher the GDP per capita, the higher the total electricity consumption it takes. In this visualisation, United States is the top one that consumes 1,013 Terawtt electricity which is double to Russia. However, many countries in Africa are experiencing lacks of electricity consumption with low GDP per capita.


Country Ranking on Coal Electricity Consumption on Years

This bump chart visualizes the country ranking on coal electricity consumption on years. The ranking is only obtained from the top 10 country. In this chart, developed countries like United States, China, India remains the top 3 that consume a lot of electricity created by coal in 20th century. On the other hand, there is fluctuation on the ranking for countries Australia and Russia over the years. However, there is a rise on the rank of South Korea. In 2016, South Korea has the sixth-largest coal electricity consumption in the world compare to 2001 which is only in rank 11. Canada was once the eleventh-highest coal electricity consumption. Until, the country has dropped out from the top 10 ranking country.
